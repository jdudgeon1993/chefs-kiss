Why items appear

Planner shortages, low stock, expiring items, and your own additions.
  • From meal plan: items needed for assigned recipes.
  • Low stock: items below your threshold.
  • Expiring soon: items within a few days of expiry.
  • New recipe ingredient: ingredients created from new recipes.
  • Custom item: anything you add manually.
